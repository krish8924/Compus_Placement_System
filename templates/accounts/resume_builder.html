Resume Tips

Your resume is often the first impression employers have of you. Make sure it's well-structured, error-free, and highlights your relevant skills and experiences.

  • Keep your resume concise and focused (1-2 pages)
  • Use bullet points to list accomplishments
  • Quantify your achievements where possible
  • Tailor your resume for each job application
  • Use PDF format for consistent formatting
